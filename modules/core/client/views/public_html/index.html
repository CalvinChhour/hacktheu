HackTheU is a 24-Hour hackathon occurring on Veteran's Day weekend.

Come and innovate with peers from across the state and nation while
networking, learning new techniques such as languages/coding for VR, and
being a part of this energetic atmosphere.

We'll have lots of hardware, mentors, and food to keep you going all weekend and attendance is free!

About Us

Our purpose is to tap into the creative potential of the University of Utah as well as the collective state.
With the growth of “Silicon Slopes”, Utah has become a burgeoning hub of innovation.
HackTheU aims to provide opportunities for growth and success that can be used to further their careers through networking, immersion, and ease of access.
